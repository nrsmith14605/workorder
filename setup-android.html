Android Setup Guide
Android Instructions

Add the Work Order App
to Your Android Phone

Follow these steps in order. The whole process takes about two minutes.

1
Open Chrome and Go to the Site
Open the Chrome browser on your Android phone. In the address bar, type chs-cs.com/workorder and tap Go.

Must be Chrome — other Android browsers may not support the full installation.

2
Log In With Your School Account
You'll see the screen below. Tap "Continue with Google" and sign in with your Warrick County Google account — the same one you use for school email.
3
Enable Notifications
After logging in you'll see your dashboard. A dark blue banner will appear at the top that says "Enable push notifications." Tap that banner.
Your phone will then ask if you want to allow notifications. Tap "Allow."

This is what lets the app alert you when a work order needs your attention — even when the app isn't open.

4
Open the Chrome Menu
Tap the three-dot menu ( ⋮ ) in the top right corner of Chrome. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home screen."
5
Choose "Install" — Not "Create Shortcut"
A dialog will appear with two options. Tap "Install" — this installs the app properly. Do not tap "Create shortcut," as that only adds a browser link and won't work the same way.
6
Confirm the Installation
A confirmation dialog will appear showing "WCSC Work Orders." Tap "Install" to finish.
7
Find the App on Your Home Screen
The Work Orders icon will appear on your Android home screen. From now on, always open the app using this icon — not by opening Chrome and going to the website.
Work Orders

You're all set! The app is installed and notifications are enabled. You'll receive alerts whenever a work order needs your attention.
