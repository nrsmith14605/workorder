Android Instructions

Add the Work Order App
to Your Android Phone

Follow these steps to install the app on your home screen and enable notifications. It takes about two minutes.

1
Open Chrome and Go to the Site
Open the Chrome browser on your Android phone. In the address bar at the top, type chs-cs.com/workorder and tap Go.
Screenshot: Chrome address bar showing chs-cs.com/workorder
2
Log In With Your School Account
Sign in using your Warrick County Google account — the same one you use for school email. You should see your work order dashboard after signing in.
Screenshot: Google sign-in screen
3
Add to Your Home Screen
Chrome may automatically show a banner at the bottom of the screen that says "Add Work Orders to Home screen." If you see it, tap it and skip to Step 5.
If you don't see a banner
Tap the three-dot menu ( ⋮ ) in the top right corner of Chrome, then tap "Add to Home screen."

The three-dot menu is always in the upper right corner of Chrome, no matter what page you're on.

Screenshot: Chrome three-dot menu open with "Add to Home screen" option highlighted
4
Tap "Install" or "Add" to Confirm
A prompt will appear asking you to confirm. Tap "Install" or "Add" — the wording may vary slightly depending on your Android version.
Screenshot: "Add to Home screen" confirmation prompt
5
Open the App From Your Home Screen
Go to your Android home screen and find the Work Orders icon. Always open the app this way — using the home screen icon instead of Chrome. Tap it now to continue setup.
Screenshot: Android home screen with Work Orders icon
6
Allow Notifications When Prompted
When the app opens, your phone will ask "Allow Work Orders to send you notifications?" Tap "Allow." This is what lets the app alert you when a work order needs your attention — even when the app isn't open.

On some Android phones, you may instead see a blue banner at the top of the app saying "Enable push notifications" — tap that banner, then tap "Allow" on the prompt that follows.

Screenshot: Android notification permission prompt
